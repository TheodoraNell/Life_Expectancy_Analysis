A Global Exploration of
Human Life Expectancy

Over the past century, human life expectancy has greatly increased due to advancements in science, technology, and cultural awareness of public health. But which factors have had the greatest impact? We believe that answering this question could help to provide key insights for countless organizations all over the world that lead humanitarian efforts, government legislation, and infrastructure planning.

The majority of the data used in this analysis comes from the World Health Organization. The datasets contain data on a wide variety of health-related and economic factors for over 200 countries during the years 2001-2017. Our goal is to create a machine learning model to accurately predict life expectancy based on these metrics and determine which features have the highest importance.



DATA SOURCES: Life Expectancy , World Health Statistics 2020
